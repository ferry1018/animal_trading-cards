Tiger

They grunt, growl, roar, moan, snarl, chuff, hiss and gasp. It's thought that each vocalisation is used to communicate different things.

  • Scientific name: Panthera tigris
  • Avergae Length: 3.3 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Native Habitats including grassland and forests

Tigers are powerful and majestic big cats known for their striking orange coat marked with dark stripes, providing effective camouflage in their natural habitats. They have a robust build, powerful limbs, and distinctive facial markings, including white spots on their ears. Tigers are solitary and territorial predators, known for their stealth and agility when hunting prey. With a keen sense of sight and hearing, they are top predators in their ecosystems, playing a crucial role in maintaining ecological balance. Unfortunately, many tiger subspecies are endangered due to habitat loss and poaching, emphasizing the importance of conservation efforts.
